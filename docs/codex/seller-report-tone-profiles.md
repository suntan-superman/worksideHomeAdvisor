# Seller Report Tone Profiles

## `prep_report`
- Practical, preparation-first, direct
- Avoid broad marketing adjectives
- Emphasize blockers, sequencing, and risk control

## `balanced_report`
- Balanced guidance with opportunity framing
- Keep prep guidance concise and actionable

## `launch_report`
- Confident and seller-reassuring
- Focus on launch consistency and final checks

## Source
Tone profiles are defined in `output-class-engine.js` and consumed by report rendering.
