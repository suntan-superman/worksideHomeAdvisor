# ROI Language Rules

ROI messaging is generated in:

- `report.payload.improvementEconomics.summary`
- `report.payload.improvementEconomics.decisionMessage`
- `report.payload.improvementEconomics.netUpside`

Rules:

- Preserve current directional calculations for estimated prep cost and value protection.
- Use seller-friendly language with cautious certainty.
- Avoid unsupported market-stat claims.
- Emphasize decision utility, not just passive metrics.

Current decision-style format:

- "A focused prep investment of about $X may protect or unlock roughly $Y in presentation-driven value."

Fallback when precision is weak:

- "Readiness signals suggest the home may be leaving value on the table until top prep items are complete."
