# Workside Home Advisor
## Provider Acquisition Playbook (Codex + GTM Ready)

Last Updated: 2026-03-29

---

# 1. Objective

Acquire high-quality local service providers (inspectors, title, photographers, cleaners, etc.) quickly and cost-effectively to seed the marketplace in Bakersfield (and future markets), while ensuring strong response rates and early ROI.

---

# 2. Core Strategy

> Start local, start curated, prove value, then scale.

Key pillars:
- Hyperlocal focus (Bakersfield first)
- Manual seeding (10–20 providers per category)
- Fast onboarding (≤3 minutes)
- Immediate lead delivery (SMS-first)
- Clear ROI for providers (leads within days)

---

# 3. Target Provider Profiles (Phase 1)

Prioritize:
- Home Inspectors
- Title Companies
- Real Estate Attorneys
- Photographers
- Cleaning / Staging

Ideal provider traits:
- Responds quickly (phone/text friendly)
- Has existing Google presence (reviews)
- Owner-operated or small team (decides fast)

---

# 4. Sourcing Providers

## 4.1 Google (Primary)
Search patterns:
- "home inspector Bakersfield"
- "real estate photographer Bakersfield"
- "title company near me"

Collect:
- business name
- phone
- website
- rating
- location

---

## 4.2 Yelp / Maps / Local Directories
Use as secondary sources.

---

## 4.3 Realtor Network (HIGH VALUE)
- Ask agents who they use
- These providers already convert well

---

# 5. Outreach Playbook

## 5.1 Cold SMS (BEST channel)

Template:
"""
Hi [Name], I run a local platform helping home sellers in Bakersfield connect with service providers like you.

We send real, ready-to-go homeowner requests (no spam lists). We’re onboarding a few providers in your area.

Want me to send you a couple sample leads this week?
"""

Goal:
- get a reply
- move to onboarding link

---

## 5.2 Phone Call (Optional High-Conversion)

Script:
- “We’re sending local homeowner requests”
- “No long contracts”
- “You can start receiving leads immediately”

---

## 5.3 Email (Secondary)

Keep short:
- 3–5 sentences max
- link to onboarding

---

# 6. Onboarding Funnel

1. Provider clicks onboarding link
2. Completes 3–5 step flow
3. Selects plan (or free trial)
4. Stripe checkout (if paid)
5. Goes live instantly
6. Receives first lead via SMS

---

# 7. Early Incentives

To accelerate adoption:

- Offer first 5–10 leads free
- Or 14-day free trial
- Or discounted first month

---

# 8. Supply/Demand Balance

IMPORTANT:

- Do NOT onboard too many providers early
- Keep:
  - ~3–5 providers per category per ZIP cluster

This ensures:
- higher lead conversion
- better provider satisfaction
- less churn

---

# 9. Lead Experience (Critical)

Providers must feel:

"These are real opportunities"

So:
- only send high-intent leads
- avoid over-dispatching
- limit to top 3 providers

---

# 10. Retention Strategy

Track:
- response speed
- acceptance rate
- engagement

If low:
- reduce their ranking
- eventually pause them

Reward:
- fast responders
- high acceptance providers

---

# 11. Provider Value Messaging

Always emphasize:

- Local homeowner leads (not junk lists)
- No cold calling required
- Simple SMS acceptance
- Fast onboarding
- Predictable monthly cost

---

# 12. Scaling Plan

## Phase 1 (Weeks 1–2)
- manually onboard 10–20 providers per category
- Bakersfield only

## Phase 2 (Weeks 3–6)
- refine messaging
- introduce paid plans
- optimize response rates

## Phase 3
- expand to nearby cities
- introduce referral program

---

# 13. Referral Loop (Later)

Ask providers:

"Know another provider who would benefit?"

Offer:
- referral bonus
- discounted month

---

# 14. Metrics to Track

- providers onboarded per week
- activation rate
- response rate
- lead acceptance %
- churn rate
- revenue per provider
